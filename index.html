真实身体科普 / Body Facts

胸部由乳腺、脂肪、结缔组织、血管和神经等组成。胸部的大小、形状和敏感程度会受到遗传、激素、体重、年龄、月经周期等影响，并不存在统一的“标准样子”。

Breasts are made up of mammary glands, fat, connective tissue, blood vessels, nerves, and other tissues. Their size, shape, and sensitivity can be influenced by genetics, hormones, body weight, age, and the menstrual cycle. There is no single “standard” appearance.

经期前后、怀孕、哺乳、压力变化、运动摩擦或内衣不合适，都可能让胸部出现胀痛、刺痛或触碰敏感。很多周期性胸痛与激素变化有关，并不等于“矫情”。

Around menstruation, during pregnancy or breastfeeding, under stress, after exercise-related friction, or when underwear does not fit well, breasts may feel swollen, sore, sharp, or sensitive to touch. Many forms of cyclical breast pain are related to hormonal changes. This does not mean someone is “being dramatic.”

但如果出现新的硬块、持续单侧疼痛、乳头异常分泌物、皮肤凹陷、明显红肿或形状突然改变，应及时寻求医生帮助。

However, if a new lump appears, if there is persistent pain on one side, unusual nipple discharge, skin dimpling, obvious redness or swelling, or a sudden change in shape, medical advice should be sought promptly.

女性真实心声 / Women’s Real Voice

我不希望胸部只被拿来评价大小、性感或是否“够女性化”。有时候它会痛、会胀、会被衣服勒到，也会让我在公共场合感到不自在。它不是别人开玩笑的对象，也不是证明我们价值的部位。它只是我身体的一部分，需要被理解，而不是被打量。

I do not want my breasts to be judged only by their size, sexiness, or whether they are “feminine enough.” Sometimes they hurt, swell, or feel restricted by clothing. They can also make me feel uncomfortable in public. They are not an object for jokes, and they are not a measure of our value. They are simply part of my body, and they deserve understanding, not staring.

请先听见身体本人的声音，再评价它。
Please listen to the person’s own body experience before judging it.
